Apple Inc. is a technology company that designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, and wearables. 
Apple’s products and services include iPhone, Mac, iPad, Apple Watch, and Apple TV, among others. 
In fiscal year 2022, Apple’s net sales exceeded $300 billion, reflecting continued growth in iPhone and services revenue.
The company also invests heavily in research and development to innovate around consumer electronics, software, and services.
